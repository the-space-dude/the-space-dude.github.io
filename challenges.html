Monthly KSP Challenge

Objective: Colonize the Mun
Budget: Unlimited
Deadline: August 31st, 2024, 11:59 PM Local Time
Rewards: Extra XP Points for Jebediah Kerman

Steps & Requirements:

  1. Launch a System of Relays
    Requirements:
    Able to maintain 24/7 communication no matter where you are
    Able to maintain a stable orbit (no collision)
  2. Launch Bases onto the Surface of the Mun
    Requirements:
    The base has to be on even ground
    At least 3 bases on the Mun
    Must be self-sustainable (has it's own power source)
    No landscape that blocks sunlight (Only if you have solar panels)
  3. Launch a Rover for Each Base
    Requirements:
    Must be within 50 meters of the base
    Must be self-sustainable
    Can fit a crew of four
  4. Launch Tourist Using Spaceplanes
    Requirements:
    Must land within 1km of the base

Bonus Step: Gather scientific data
