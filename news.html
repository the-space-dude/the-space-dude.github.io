  • SpaceX First Falcon 9 Failure in 8 Years

    SpaceX attempted to launched 20 starlink satelites into orbit, but during its second stage burn, the engine failured to complete the burn. The FAA grounded SpaceX to any launches unless they figure out the cause for this failure.
